# CloudDesk — Project Management SaaS

## About Us

CloudDesk is a project management platform for teams of all sizes. Plan projects, track tasks, collaborate in real-time, and ship faster. Available on web, iOS, and Android.

Website: clouddesk.io
Support hours: 24/7 via chat and email. Phone support available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST.

---

## Plans & Pricing

We offer three plans:

Free Plan ($0/month):
- Up to 5 team members
- 3 projects
- 500 MB storage
- Basic task management
- Community support

Pro Plan ($12/user/month, billed annually or $15/month):
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited projects
- 50 GB storage
- Gantt charts, time tracking, custom fields
- Priority email support (response within 4 hours)

Enterprise Plan (custom pricing, contact sales):
- Everything in Pro
- SSO/SAML authentication
- Audit logs
- 99.9% SLA
- Dedicated account manager
- On-premise deployment option

All plans include a 14-day free trial of Pro features. No credit card required for the trial.

To upgrade your plan, go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Changes take effect immediately. You will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle.

To downgrade, go to the same page and select a lower plan. The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will not be charged again until the downgrade is processed.

## Billing & Payments

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. We also support wire transfers for Enterprise plans.

Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month and sent to the billing email on your account. You can view and download all invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoice History.

If a payment fails, we will retry 3 times over 7 days. If all retries fail, your account will be downgraded to the Free plan. Your data is preserved for 30 days.

To update your payment method, go to Settings > Billing > Payment Method. Changes apply to your next invoice.

Refund policy: We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase if you are not satisfied. After 30 days, refunds are prorated. To request a refund, contact support with your account email and reason.

## Account & Security

To create an account, visit clouddesk.io/signup. You can sign up with email or use Google/GitHub SSO.

To reset your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email. The link expires in 1 hour.

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available for all plans. Go to Settings > Security > Enable 2FA. We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS.

To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone. If you are the only owner of a workspace, you must transfer ownership first.

Session management: You can view all active sessions at Settings > Security > Active Sessions. Click "Revoke" to sign out of a specific device.

## Projects & Tasks

To create a project, click "+ New Project" from the sidebar. Choose a template (Kanban, Scrum, or Blank) and invite team members.

Tasks can be organized in lists, boards (Kanban), or timeline (Gantt) views. Switch between views using the tabs at the top of any project.

Task fields: Each task has a title, description, assignee, due date, priority (low/medium/high/urgent), status, tags, and custom fields (Pro plan).

Subtasks: You can add subtasks to any task. Subtasks have their own assignee and due date. Parent task progress is calculated from subtask completion.

Comments: Team members can comment on tasks. Use @mentions to notify specific people. Attach files up to 25 MB per comment.

Due date reminders: Automatic reminders are sent 24 hours and 1 hour before a task is due. You can customize reminder timing in Settings > Notifications.

## Integrations

CloudDesk integrates with the following tools:

Slack: Get task notifications in Slack channels. Set up at Settings > Integrations > Slack. You can choose which events trigger notifications (task created, completed, assigned, commented).

GitHub: Link pull requests to tasks. When a PR is merged, the linked task is automatically marked as complete. Set up at Settings > Integrations > GitHub.

Google Calendar: Sync task due dates to your Google Calendar. Enable at Settings > Integrations > Google Calendar.

Zapier: Connect CloudDesk to 3,000+ apps via Zapier. We support triggers (task created, completed, assigned) and actions (create task, update task, add comment).

API: Full REST API available for Pro and Enterprise plans. Documentation at docs.clouddesk.io/api. API keys can be generated at Settings > API Keys. Rate limit: 100 requests per minute.

Webhooks: Set up webhooks to receive real-time notifications when events happen in your workspace. Configure at Settings > Integrations > Webhooks.

## Collaboration

Real-time collaboration: Multiple team members can view and edit the same project simultaneously. Changes are synced in real-time.

Workspace roles:
- Owner: Full access, billing management, can delete workspace
- Admin: Full access except billing, can manage members
- Member: Can create and edit projects and tasks
- Guest: Read-only access to specific projects they are invited to

Sharing: Projects can be shared with external guests via email invitation. Guests get read-only access and do not count toward your team member limit.

Activity feed: Every project has an activity feed showing all changes (task created, moved, assigned, completed, commented). Available at the project level and workspace level.

## Mobile App

CloudDesk is available on iOS (requires iOS 15 or later) and Android (requires Android 10 or later).

Download from the App Store or Google Play Store by searching "CloudDesk".

The mobile app supports all core features: view projects, create/edit tasks, comment, upload files, and receive push notifications.

## Data & Privacy

Data hosting: All data is hosted on AWS (US-East and EU-West regions). Enterprise customers can choose their preferred region.

Backups: We perform automated daily backups. Data is retained for 30 days. Enterprise customers can request custom backup schedules.

Data export: You can export all your data at any time. Go to Settings > Account > Export Data. Export includes projects, tasks, comments, and attachments in JSON format. The export is ready within 24 hours and available for download for 7 days.

GDPR: We are fully GDPR compliant. You can request data deletion by contacting privacy@clouddesk.io. We process deletion requests within 30 days.

SOC 2 Type II: We are SOC 2 Type II certified. Audit reports are available for Enterprise customers upon request.

## Troubleshooting

If the app is slow or not loading:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser (we support Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
3. Disable browser extensions that might interfere
4. Check our status page at status.clouddesk.io

If you cannot receive emails from CloudDesk:
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Add support@clouddesk.io to your contacts
3. Verify your email address in Settings > Account

If file uploads fail:
- Maximum file size is 25 MB per file (100 MB for Enterprise)
- Supported formats: images, PDFs, documents, spreadsheets, zip files
- If you exceed your storage limit, you need to upgrade your plan or delete existing files
